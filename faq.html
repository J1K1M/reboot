Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What character should I play?

Play whatever character you enjoy the most. Think about whether you'll enjoying playing this character after 5th job.

Q: What is the strongest character right now?

Right now it seems Hayato is what everyone is going for as the strongest character in Reboot. Check this DPM chart from Korean Maplestory to get a general idea of the strength of some characters.

Q: What character should I meso farm with?

Kanna is the best character to meso farm with. Their link skill and character card is also very good, so levelling a kanna should be one of your top priorities at least to level 120.

Q: Where is the best place to meso farm?

Since the nerf to Blackgate, the go-to place to meso farm is now Ghost Ship 1, 2 and 5. With Ghost Ship 1 and 2 being the best. Be warned these maps are very crowded, expect to see some macro/bot users. Alternatively you can go to Longest ride to bye bye station or Ulu Estate II in Singapore

Q: Is meso% potential the same as drop% potential?

No they are not

Q: How do I get meso%/drop% lines on my equipment?

You can only get meso% and drop% potential lines on accessories equips on Legendary tier. The max total meso% you can have is 120%, five accessories with 20% mesos obtained and 20% mesos obtained on your legendary Inner Ability. Unlike meso% you can have drop% on all your accessories coming to a total of 180% (note this is not the max total you can get), however you can get double drop% lines which will add up to your total drop rate.

Q: What are link skills?

Link skills are extra skills you can have on a character (12 at max), this is provided from other characters that you have on your account. You can get level 1 link skill at level 70 then level 2 and 3 link skill at level 120 and 210 respectively. Note that some characters do not have link skills or some only have link skills that do not reach level 3. For a more detailed guide on this please refer to Ayuimiloves guide on link skills.
