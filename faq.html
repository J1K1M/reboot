Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: What character should I play?

Play whatever character you enjoy the most. Think about whether you'll enjoying playing this character after 5th job.

Q: What is the strongest character right now?

Right now Hayato is the strongest character in Reboot. Check this DPM chart from Korean Maplestory to get a general idea of the strength of some characters.

Q: What are link skills?

Please refer to Ayuimiloves guide on link skills.

Q: What are character cards?

Please refer to Ayuimiloves guide on character cards.

Q: Where can I buy cubes from?

You can buy master craftsmans cubes from the general store in Henesys market and in Leafre. You can also buy red cubes and black cubes from the cash shop with mesos or with maple points (limited stock).

Q: Where can I get epic potential scrolls?

Although you cannot buy epic potential scrolls with mesos you can get them by doing Yu Garden dailies. /u/amityv has given a very well written guide on this, please check that out here. You also have a chance of getting them from boxes that drop from elite bosses.

Meso Farming Questions

Q: What character should I meso farm with?

Kanna is the best character to meso farm with. Their link skill and character card is also very good, so levelling a kanna should be one of your top priorities at least to level 120.

Q: Where is the best place to meso farm?

Since the nerf to Blackgate, the go-to place to meso farm is now Ghost Ship 1, 2 and 5. With Ghost Ship 1 and 2 being the best. Be warned these maps are very crowded, expect to see some macro/bot users. Alternatively you can go to Longest ride to bye bye station or Ulu Estate II in Singapore

Q: Is meso% potential the same as drop% potential?

No they are not.

Q: How do I get meso%/drop% lines on my equipment?

You can only get meso% and drop% potential lines on accessory equips on Legendary tier. The max total meso% you can have is 120%, five accessories with 20% mesos obtained and 20% mesos obtained on your legendary Inner Ability. Unlike meso% you can have drop% on all your accessories coming to a total of 180% (note this is not the max total you can get), however you can get double drop% lines which will add up to your total drop rate.

Q: Do I need to meso farm?

If you are looking to fight end game bosses and progress after hitting level 200 it is encouraged to meso farm to speed up your progression. Alternatively you can do daily bosses such as Zakuum, Hilla, N/CHT, and Pink Bean to net a fair amount of mesos.
