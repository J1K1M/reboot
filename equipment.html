Equipment Guide

The levels written are not always the level requirement to wear the equips but "realistic" levels when you should be looking to upgrade to new equips.

Armour

Level 1-140: Just wear whatever drops on the ground that gives more damage than what you are wearing.

Level 100+: Wings of Fate, Best in Slot cape until Tyrants.

Level 140+: Pensalir set, farm the last map of Korean Folk Town

Level 150+: Pensalir set with Wings of Fate and Japanese weapon (craft it or get it from Ranmaru drops)

Level 160+: Sengoku set, get belt, ring and shoulder from doing the Sengoku questline
Sweetwater weapon and gloves, Commerci questline and Commerci Solo and Party Quests.

Level 170+: Aim for CRA set (unrealistic unless), ask for carries or join a guild, need to do 10 runs of Root Abyss bosses

Level 180+: Tyrants to replace WoF, belt and boots.

Level 200+: Absolab, honestly...stick with CRA set until 230ish unless you have a large guild that can carry you Damien, Lotus.

Level 240+: Arcane gear, Lucid, ok

Accessories

Bit tricky this one, since there have been BiS rings from past events which are no longer obtainable (but may come back in the future)

Level 1-250: Henehoe set, sky blue chair, transparent equips, *the hair that every Night Lord has*, snowflake dome ring.
